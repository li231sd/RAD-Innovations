PROGRAMS

What do we do? We teach. We mentor. We build.

We fuel the next generation of engineers with the resources and mentors they need to succeed.

Rocketry

Rockets: Simulate. Build. Launch.

This involves learning to simulate & build high-powered and low-powered model rockets, including the software and physics behind model rockets.

View Classes

Aeromodelling

Planes: Imagine. Design. Fly.

Learn to use professional software to design your creations, apply the four forces of flight, and bring projects to life that you’ve only dreamed of!

View Classes

Drones

Drones: Build. Fly. Explore.

Learn to build real drones such as FPV and quadcopters from scratch, gaining valuable skills like soldering along the way. Not into building? Learn to fly them and have fun while doing it!

Q & A

Who can join this program?

We take members who are in high school and beyond. For schools where high school starts in 8th grade, we only accept students from 9th grade and above.

What is the cost?

Membership starts at $10 per year for basic access. Program-specific kits range from $20 to $300 depending on the project.

Who are we?

We are a community of passionate mentors and builders. All mentors are volunteers, and we take no profit from the work we do here.
