Drones Programs

Explore the phases of drone training: learning, advanced certification, and technician courses.

Drones — Part 1

Learning Phase

What you will learn:

  • The theory behind drones
  • Usage & development (theory)
  • Practice flying drones
  • Master your own obstacle course

Prerequisites: 9th–12th grade (recommended 9th–10th)

Program Details: Cost: Based off project, Duration: 4 classes/month for 3 months

Certification: RAD certification exam required

Drones — Part 2

Advanced Drones

What you will learn:

  • FAA regulations
  • Scientific principles of VTOL
  • Specialty drone flight practice
  • Prepare for FAA Part 107 exam

Prerequisites: 12th grade and higher

Program Details: Cost: Based off project, Duration: 20 theory + 10 practical classes (1–1.5 years)

Certification: FAA Commercial Drone Pilot license

Drones — Part 3

Technician Course

What you will learn:

  • Build mission-based drones
  • Fly drones you build
  • Hands-on design-driven learning

Prerequisites: High school or higher (recommended 11th–12th)

Program Details: Cost: Project-based (includes materials)

Duration: Based on project
