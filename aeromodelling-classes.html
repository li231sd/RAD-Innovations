Aeromodelling Programs

Explore the phases of aeromodelling: learning, advanced aeromodelling, and start some long-term projects.

Aeromodelling — Stage 1

Learning Phase

What you will learn:

  • Physics behind airplanes
  • Development process & aerodynamics
  • Create a model airplane from a kit

Prerequisites: Students in 9th and 10th grade

About the class: Cost: Based off project, Duration: 4 classes/month for 2 months

Competitions: Small competitions, Oshkosh Aeromodelling Competition

Aeromodelling — Stage 2

Advanced

What you will learn:

  • Build your airplane in software
  • Learn about jet engines
  • Different aerodynamics & designs
  • Build an RC Plane!

Prerequisites: Students in 11th grade and higher

About the class: Cost: Based off project, Duration: 4 classes/month for 6 months

Competitions: AMA, Oshkosh Airshow

Certifications: RAD certification requires a test
