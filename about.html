Hima Shah

Founder & AEO/NAR Mentor

Hima Shah is an entrepreneur, aerospace mentor, STEM educator, and business advisor with a lifelong passion for aviation, innovation, and education. She holds a B.Com from Gujarat University and earned her Private Pilot License through the cadet training program Indian Air Force Air Wing Cadet Training Program.

Along with RAD Innovations, Hima owns Om Innovations, a company that sells all new innovative products on Amazon, Etsy, Ebay, and Shopify.

Her vision through RAD Innovations is to prepare young innovators to dream big, build fearlessly, and lead confidently in science, technology, and aerospace.
