Rocketry Programs

Explore the phases of rocketry: learning, advanced rockets, and long-term projects.

Rocketry — Part 1

Learning Phase

What you will learn:

  • The basic principles of rocketry
  • Science behind rocketry
  • Open Rocket Software
  • Build a model rocket! (mid-power rocketry)

Prerequisite: Must be a high schooler (9th - 12th grade)

Program Details: Cost: Based off project, Duration: 4 classes/month for 3 months.

Certification: ALL STUDENTS required to take RAD certification exam.

Rocketry — Part 2

Advanced Rockets

What you will learn:

  • Advanced rockets
  • Get licensed for high-power L1, L2, L3 rockets
  • Open Rocket & Rocket Sim software
  • Technicalities & missions of payloads

Prerequisite: Must be in 11th grade or higher

Program Details: Cost: Based off project (including materials), Duration: 4 classes/month for 3 months.

Certification: NAR Certification

Competitions: NASA SLI, TARC

Rocketry — Part 3

Long Term Projects

What you will learn:

  • Anything as you make your project
  • Make innovations

Prerequisite: Must be a high schooler (9th - 12th grade)

Program Details: Cost & Duration depend on the project
